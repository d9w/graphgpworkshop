Call for Papers

Submitter papers must not exceed 4 pages, excluding references, and must follow GECCO style guidelines.

The content of the paper must be either new original work or discuss new perspectives on recently published work. Submitting work that is in an early stage or in progress is welcomed and appreciated.

Papers must be submitted using the GECCO submission site. After login, the authors need to select the “Workshop Paper” submission form.

All accepted papers will be presented at the corresponding workshop and appear in the GECCO Conference Companion Proceedings. As detailed in the program, all accepted works will be presented during a poster sesssion of the workshop.

For more informations check GECCO workshop page.
